# Expense Tracked App

## ExpenseTracked is a online service which allows you to be able tokeep track of daily, monthly and yearly payments and expenses

The project is aimed at developing an online service that provides a comprehensive solution for managing personal or business finances. The service will offer users the ability to keep track of their daily, monthly, and yearly payments and expenses in a convenient and efficient manner. Additionally, the service will include a chat room feature that allows users to connect with their peers for financial advice and support, as well as a booking form for scheduling consultations with financial experts.

The online service will have a user-friendly interface that allows users to easily input and categorize their payments and expenses. Users will be able to set up recurring payments, track their spending habits, and generate reports to gain insights into their financial health. The service will also provide reminders for upcoming payments, helping users stay organized and avoid late fees or penalties.

The chat room feature will enable users to connect with other users who have similar financial goals or interests. Users will be able to exchange tips, share experiences, and seek advice on various financial topics. The chat room will be moderated to ensure a safe and respectful environment for all users.

In addition to the chat room, the online service will offer a booking form for scheduling consultations with financial experts. Users will be able to request appointments for one-on-one consultations with certified financial planners or advisors. The consultation service will provide personalized financial advice tailored to the individual needs and goals of the users.

The online service will prioritize user privacy and security. It will use encryption and other security measures to protect users' financial data and personal information. The service will also comply with relevant data protection regulations, such as GDPR, to ensure user trust and confidence.

The project will be developed using modern web technologies, with a responsive design that ensures a seamless user experience across various devices, including desktops, tablets, and smartphones. The service will be accessible 24/7, allowing users to manage their finances at their convenience.

Overall, the online service will provide a comprehensive solution for managing payments and expenses, connecting with peers in a chat room, and booking consultations with financial experts. It will empower users to take control of their finances, make informed financial decisions, and achieve their financial goals with confidence.
